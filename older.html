Get Out There!

Here are a few great things you can do in the greater Denver area as a young adult!






For the college student


Ages 18-34

Young Invincibles!

Find your voice in your local legislative process and make changes in the things that matter to you!

Take Me There!
Young Invincibles is a non-profit with the aim to make sure this generations young adults are not only heard but also active in the political process. They value diversity in background, opinion, ambitions, location-everything. They are there to find and give the voices of everyone-not just a small few. With projects and legislative meetings you get a chance to make a difference in your community and learn what it means to work in the legislative field.


Ages 13+

PopCultureCon

Volunteer and spend time around all the geeky things you love!

Take Me There!
Love anything fandom and have a little extra time in the summer? The next time it rolls around, volunteer for Denver Pop Culture Con! Volunteer half of a day and spend the other half enjoying the exhibitions, panels and art. Denver Pop Culture Con is presented by Pop Culture Classroom-a nonprofit with the goal to help improve literacy through alternative approaches like art and board games. (So it's very family friendly!) Give yourself a chance to learn new things through your team as you volunteer and enjoy yourself.
